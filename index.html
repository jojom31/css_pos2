MY POEMS

BY. MS. JOJO

CONSISTENCY

Consistency
You don’t know what you did to me
You showed a side that allowed you in
And in again
Because you were consistent
In the beginning, now its different
Not consistent
But once again you’re in
How do I let this mend
I can’t because once again
Consistent in your inconsistency
Which I relentlessly wonder
Is it worth it
I know I’m worth it
But are you worth it
It being my consistency
-JoJo

IF

If you allow the past in your present
Your future is nonexistent
How can you be persistent
Or even consistent
If you can’t allow the past distance
From the old that continues to scold you
Not mold you into the person
You want to be
The past can sometimes become a faded memory
You maybe holding on willingly
The present is the focus
If you are always worrying about
What’s gonna happen or
What’s supposed to happen
You miss out on
Whats happening right now
Don’t wait around
Embrace life
Get past this life
Of regret
And be met with peace
Let it go so you can grow
I know it’s hard it’s just that feeling
Of something new
Oh look you grew
Look around so much has changed
You blocked the past from your present
Now your future exists
You overcame that fear of what if
-JoJo
